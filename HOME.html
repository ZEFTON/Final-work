CONCERNED YOUTH FOR NATURE INITIATIVE

Concerned Youth For Nature Initiative (CONYi) is a grass root initiative run by young people working creatively and innovatively to end plastic pollution through education and community service projects.

OUR GOAL

By 2050 there should be no more plastic waste in the oceans, seas,
no more plastic waste in our lakes, rivers, forests, parks and streets.

YOU CAN START TODAY

Use less plastic everyday and pick up a piece of plastic waste every day from the streets, beach or waterfront.
Tell your family, friends and colleagues about the problem and join us.

Plastics are now a global threat to human life as they are now in our human food chain.
The initiative seeks to create awareness and education about plastics that can result into:

  • Action oriented activities that can fight and end plastic pollution.
  • Encourage climate conservation.
  • Protection of biodiversity.
  • Enhancing peace.
  • Providing employment opportunities that improve livelihood.
